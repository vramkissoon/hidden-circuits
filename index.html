Hidden Circuits

A literary investigation

I want to create a website that documents my exploration of and research in the topics of embedded systems, malware, neuroscience, and human economies. I want it to showcase my skills and provide a platform for learning. The vision seems fragmented, I know, but it is unified in some way that is hard to articulate. Right now, however, I have one idea that seems to underpin it all: electricity.
